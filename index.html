About Asha Trust

Asha Trust Kanpur is a non-profit organization committed to improving the quality of life for underprivileged communities. Through educational initiatives, health awareness programs, vocational training, and community development projects, the trust strives to create sustainable social change. Our mission is to provide equal opportunities and inspire hope among individuals and families.

Our Main Goals

Education

Providing educational support, scholarships, digital literacy, and learning resources for children and youth.

Sports

Sports which make a child more dedicable and force them to maintain his focus on one thing.

Skill Development

Vocational training, computer education, entrepreneurship, and employment support.

Women Empowerment

Programs focused on self-help groups, leadership, financial literacy, and livelihood opportunities.
